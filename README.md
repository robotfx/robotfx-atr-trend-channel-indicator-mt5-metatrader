The <strong>ATR Trend Channel indicator for MetaTrader 5</strong> tries to encapsulate the price within 6 bands, determined by the <strong>ATR indicator</strong> and its relationship with the **SMA (simple moving average)**.

<div style="text-align: center;"><h3><a href="#" id="Functions" name="Functions" title="ATR Trend Channel Functions">ATR Trend Channel Functions</a></h3></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjQfE3OAk_zE6nB-_-DCN4lQJBel6P_lmr-UZyppzbpG30_xP6Qb3lYm126dM-LD0J51dQh_coSIRo2UPuMWKkOTbCHz5BeMDrbzgmWsDtmxzdRZi738cJdQ6FIyGmjoZ3NhCTQtHR6vy_wd3sHxUYZepcQco3CL7tzuvIKG1hsPdXM5GZ33vbGZ_ElF14q/s1916/atr.png" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img alt="A chart showing the ATR Trend Channel indicator's six bands on a MetaTrader 5 chart." border="0" data-original-height="826" data-original-width="1916" height="276" loading="lazy" src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/b/R29vZ2xl/AVvXsEjQfE3OAk_zE6nB-_-DCN4lQJBel6P_lmr-UZyppzbpG30_xP6Qb3lYm126dM-LD0J51dQh_coSIRo2UPuMWKkOTbCHz5BeMDrbzgmWsDtmxzdRZi738cJdQ6FIyGmjoZ3NhCTQtHR6vy_wd3sHxUYZepcQco3CL7tzuvIKG1hsPdXM5GZ33vbGZ_ElF14q/w640-h276-e90-rw/atr.png" title="ATR Trend Channel indicator for MetaTrader 5" width="640"/></a></div><br/><p>On the chart, it might look similar to the Guppy indicator.
</p>
<p>The 2 middle lines are determined by the ATR*multiplier, the seconds up/down are determined by the ATR*multiplier plus the high/low SMA and the outer channel lines are the 2*ATR*multiplier + SMA (High or Low depending on the whether there is the upper band or the lower one).</p>
<p>One trading strategy idea using this **ATR indicator** is to focus on entering a trade when the price reverses while continuing with the trading, after crossing at least the middle bands. If the price pokes through the opposite outer band – for example if there is a downtrend and the price swings higher then the upper band – a change of trend might happen, and the trader should trade with caution or wait for the new trend to form.</p>
